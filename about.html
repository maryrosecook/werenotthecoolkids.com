We're Not The Cool Kids are a band from Brixton who make music on their computer. Mary does the beats, plays the guitar, wrangles Ableton Live, stumbles over the keyboard, screams and sings harmonies with herself.

We're Not The Cool Kids sing about one's place in the world, hospital, unrequited love, being queer, loneliness, physiology, betrayal and hope.
